GeoEasy 3.0.1

surveying calculation, network adjustment, digital terrain models, regression calculation

History

The beginning of GeoEasy goes back to the late nineties (1997). Before the 3.0 version it was a propriarety software marketed in Hungary. After twenty year long development (with active and less active periods) in 2017 the license has been changed to open source.
